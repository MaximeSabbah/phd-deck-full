Optimal Control Problem

\[ \begin{split} & \min_{x,u} & \sum_{t=0}^{N-1}l(x_t,u_t) + l_{term}(x_N) \\ & ~ \text{s.t.} & ~ x_{t+1}=f(x_t,u_t) \\ \end{split} \]
$ x=\begin{pmatrix} q \\ \dot{q} \end{pmatrix} $ $u: \textit{torque}$

No constraints penalties in $l(x,u)$

- Mastalli et al. (2020), Crocoddyl: An Efficient and Versatile Framework for Multi-Contact Optimal Control, ICRA

[PART_NAME_I]

Optimal Control Problem

Feasibility-driven Differential Dynamic Programming

Output:
  • $u_0$
  • $x_0$
  • $K_0 = \left.\dfrac{\partial u}{\partial x}\right|_{x_0}$
Locally optimal policy : $u_t = u_0 + K_0(x_t - x_0)$

Cost Function

Cost function:
encodes all the information about the desired behavior of the robot
$l(x,u) = w_\mathit{cons}l_\mathit{cons} + w_\mathit{eq}l_\mathit{eq} + w_\mathit{goal}l_\mathit{goal} + w_\mathit{reg}l_\mathit{reg} $
Weights to enforce practical priorities:
$w_\mathit{cons}\gg w_\mathit{eq}\gg w_\mathit{goal}\gg w_\mathit{reg}$
- Dantec et al. (2021), Whole Body Model Predictive Control with a Memory of Motion: Experiments on a Torque-Controlled Talos, ICRA

[PART_NAME_I]

Cost Function

$l_\mathit{cons}$ Constraints:
  • Joints positions
  • Joints velocities
  • Joints torques
$l_\mathit{eq}$ Equilibrium:
  • Position of the Center Of Mass
$l_\mathit{goal}$ Task:
  • Position of the End-Effector
  • Orientation of the End-Effector
  • Velocity of the End-Effector
$|p_{EF} - p_{target}|$
$l_\mathit{reg}$ Regularization:
  • State (Joints positions and velocities)
  • Torque
$|x_t - x_{ref}|_w$
