Introduction

Human biomechanical model

Gepetto Mannequin:

  • Built on ISB recommendations1
  • Scaled using anthropometric tables2
  • 36 actuated DoFs + floating base at the pelvis
  • Model boundaries (joint positions, velocities, torques) derived from literature3,4
  • Available in open-source under the URDF format5
1 G. Wu et al., ISB recommendation on definitions of joint coordinate systems of various joints [...], Journal of Biomechanics, 2022-2025.
2 R. Dumas et al., Adjustments to McConville et al. and Young et al. body segment inertial parameters, Journal of Biomechanics, 2007.
3 D. Boone and S. Azen, Normal range of motion of joints in male subjects, JBJS, 1979.
4 T. Robert et al., Estimation of external contact loads using an inverse dynamics and optimization approach: [...] maneuvers, Journal of Biomechanics, 2013.
5 https://github.com/Gepetto/example-robot-data
