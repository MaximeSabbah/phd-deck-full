Optimal Control Problem

\[ \begin{split} & \min_{X,U} & \quad \Phi_{tot}(X,U) = \omega_\mathit{meas}\Phi_\mathit{meas}(X,U) \\ & & \quad + \omega_\mathit{reg}\Phi_\mathit{reg}(X,U) \\ & ~ \text{s.t.} & ~ \mathbf{g}(X,U) = 0 \ (\text{dynamics}) & \\ & & ~ \mathbf{h}(X,U) \leq 0 \ (\text{boundaries}) & \\ \end{split} \]
$ X = \begin{pmatrix} q_n \\ \dot{q}_n \end{pmatrix} $ $U=(\ddot{q}_n)$ ${n \in [\![0,N-1]\!]}$
J. Andersson et al., CasADi: a software framework for nonlinear optimization and optimal control, Mathematical Programming, 2019
L. Vanroye et al., Fatrop: A fast constrained optimal control problem solver for robot trajectory optimization and control, IEEE IROS, 2023
