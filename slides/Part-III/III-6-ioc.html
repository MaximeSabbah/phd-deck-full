Inverse Optimal Control

Let's consider \(\mathcal{Y}^* = (X^*, U^*)\)  as the optimal motion demonstrated by an individual:
\[ \begin{split} & \min_{\boldsymbol{\omega}} & \quad \lvert \lvert \mathcal{Y}^* - \hat{\mathcal{Y}}(\boldsymbol{\omega}) \rvert \rvert^2 \\ \end{split} \]
\[ \begin{split} \hat{\mathcal{Y}}(\boldsymbol{\omega}) = & \ \text{arg}\min_{\mathcal{Y}} & \quad \Phi_{tot}(\mathcal{Y},\boldsymbol{\omega}) \\ & ~ \text{s.t.} & ~ \mathbf{g}(\mathcal{Y}) = 0 \ (\text{dynamics}) \\ & & ~ \mathbf{h}(\mathcal{Y}) \leq 0 \ (\text{boundaries}) \\ \end{split} \]
